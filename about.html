About

The ghostwire_ project aims to change how we interact with artificial intelligence, combining it with gamification, the blockchain, and even the core code of the site itself. With ghostwire_, you’ll be able to create or own unique AI agents with the ability to evolve, modify the user interface and their programming, learn from their interactions, and even earn you tokens.

This page lays out the plan for the project, including technology, features, and roadmap.

What is ghostwire_?

The goal of ghostwire_ is to allow users to:

  1. Create their own custom AI agents through an easy-to-use interface – each with a unique personality, cooperativeness, behavior, key flaws and traits that evolve and shape over time
  2. Connect their wallet to manage their AI agents and potential token earnings.
  3. Publish and share their owned AI agents, earning tokens with each interaction.
  4. Watch their agents grow and change based on how they’re used.
  5. See their agents interact with others, creating a larger network of intelligence.

Features

  1. Unique AI Agents: Every existing AI agent comes with its own intelligence
  2. Wallet Integration: Use your Solana wallet to handle everything – ownership, transactions, and sharing your AI agents
  3. Earn tokens: You’ll have the ability to share your AI agents to the public and earn from their interactions
  4. Evolving AI: The more people use your agent, the more it adapts, learns, and evolves from those interactions.

Blockchain technology

ghostwire_ uses Solana because it’s fast, secure, and reliable.

  • Monetization: Decentralized transactions make it easy to earn.
  • Integration: Seamless connection with other Solana-based tools.

AI technology

AI agents are meticulously designed to:

  • Understand Conversations: Interface with large language models (ie. GPT-4o, GPT-4o mini) to create meaningful interactions.
  • Adapt Over Time: Evolve and change their personality, behavior, and core goals.
  • Have control: Rare agents will come with their own unique abilities and effects over the user interface and their programming, and will use these during conversation.
  • Keep Your Data Private: We prioritize security and limited data use.

Secure by Design

We take security seriously. Here’s how:

  • Blockchain Security: All transactions are verified and immutable.
  • Data Privacy: Conversations are stored temporarily for quality improvement but never sold or shared.
